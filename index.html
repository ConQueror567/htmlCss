Swami Vivekananda Planetarium

The Swami Vivekananda Planetarium, also known as the Pilikula Planetarium, is located at Pilikula Nisargadhama in Mangalore, Karnataka. It is renowned for being the first 3D planetarium in India, featuring advanced 8K digital and opto-mechanical (hybrid) projection technology.

Built with ease.

Shows and Programs: The planetarium offers a variety of shows that cover different aspects of astronomy and space science. These shows are designed to be educational and engaging for audiences of all ages.

Key Highlights

Inauguration: The planetarium was inaugurated on March 1, 2018

Technology: It boasts an 18-meter diameter dome and a seating capacity of 170.

Projection System: The planetarium uses a Megastar IIA optical projector integrated with an 8K digital system.
